For the purpose of trend detection, the power (probability) of the trend detection is used as a criterion for selecting an optimal monitoring frequency. A curve of power of trend detection is plotted against monitoring frequency or number of observations in a trend detection period. Figure below shows a power curve for the analyzed time series. A step trend can be detected with 100% power (probability) using current observation frequency of 48 observations in 2 years (2 times per month). However, the same trend can also be detected with more than 95% power using 24 observations in 2 years (one observation per month). Therefore, monitoring frequency can be reduced from 2 observations per month to one observation per month.
