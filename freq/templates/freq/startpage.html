Time series analysis are applied for monitoring the impact of abstraction and climate change on groundwater levels. This tool help to understand temporal changes of groundwater levels. The time series analysis consist of detection of trends; estimation of periodic component an autoregressive model. Based on this information it also provides an estimate on the monitoring sampling frequency, which is one of the key components of good groundwater monitoring network design.
