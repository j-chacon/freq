A trend shows a long-term change in the mean level. Both linear and step trend are commonly observed in groundwater time series. Linear trend should be applied when there is a gradual increase or decrease of groundwater levels where a step trend should be applied when there is a sudden increase or decrease in groundwater level.

The trend is removed from the time series in order to continue with determination of periodic fluctuations and autoregressive model.
